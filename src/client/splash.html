HOW TO PLAY

The Idea

A hidden fleet of 6 ships is placed on a 10×10 grid — the same layout for every player, every day. Find and sink the whole fleet using as few bombs and hints as possible.

The Fleet

Each ship has a real shape: Carrier (L), Battleship (square), Destroyer (Z), Submarine (straight), Patrol Boat (corner), and Scout (domino). You know the shapes going in, but never their position or rotation.

Bombing

Tap a cell to bomb it. You get 30 bombs total. Each one is a miss, a hit, or the finishing blow that sinks a ship.

Hints

Stuck? Use a hint to instantly reveal one cell of a ship you haven't found yet — it's free!

Scoring

Bigger ships are worth more: 10 points per cell sunk. Each hint used costs 15 points. Bonus points for each bomb remaining !! Compare your score on the daily leaderboard.

Every Day

New fleet, new layout, every day. Come back tomorrow for a fresh puzzle.
